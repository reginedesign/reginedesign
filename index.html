About

Hi! My name is Regine Tan, I am from Singapore and currently studying in Republic Polytechnic pursuing Diploma in Interactive And Digital Media.

I like to draw when I have my free time, and I have been doodling ever since I was young. What made me came to enjoy drawing started with cartoon shows that I would wake up every morning just to watch. One of my most memorable cartoon that inspired me to start drawing was Pokemon, and till today, I still draw my favourite cartoon.

Besides drawing, I do have a interest in the field of Animation. This interest let me to my current status. I have brushed up my skills and refine my school work to be of industrial standard, that I hope will be feature in future movies or shows. This is only what I can achieve in school. Just imagine what I can achieve in the industry. Here is a philosophy that I live by “Everybody has a unique style. Art is subjective to the eye of the beholder.”
